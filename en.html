Türkçe

Mosaic Cake

with ready-made biscuits

∵∵∵∵∵∵∵∵∵∵∵∵

Ingredients

⁘ 1 egg

⁘ 1 cup of milk

⁘ 1 cup of sugar

⁘ 1 tablespoon cacao

⁘ 1/2 package of margarine

⁘ 300 g petit beurre biscuits

⁘ Crushed hazelnuts or pistachios

Preparation

⁘ Melt the margarine in the steam of boiling water and add the sugar.

⁘ When the sugar is melted add milk, cacao, and egg.

⁘ Crumble the biscuits into small pieces.

⁘ Add cracked hazelnuts and / or pistachios.

⁘ Mix them all.

⁘ Pour the mixture into baking paper and give it the shape you desire.

⁘ Freeze it in the freezer, unpack it from the baking paper and serve.
